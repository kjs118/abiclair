ABOUT ABI

Immerse yourself in the mesmerizing sounds of Lafayette's very own singer-songwriter-guitarist, Abi Clair. With a voice that resonates with pure emotion and guitar melodies that flow effortlessly, her performances are nothing short of captivating. She wrote her first song at 8 years old and picked up the guitar at age 11, drawing inspiration from artists like Led Zeppelin, Oasis, The Beatles, and Paramore. Abi Clair brings a fresh perspective to each song at her shows and invites you to personalize your experience by "ordering" from her unique Song Menu, which includes over 100 artists and songs! Don't miss the chance to witness the passion of Abi Clair live as she performs timeless covers and original music all throughout Acadiana!
